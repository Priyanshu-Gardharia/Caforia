Privacy Policy

At Caforia, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Personal Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email, delivery address, phone number, and payment information when you make a purchase, sign up for an account, or interact with our services.

Additionally, we collect data automatically, such as browser type, IP address, device information, and browsing behavior through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders.
  • Send you order confirmations, updates, and customer support responses.
  • Improve user experience by analyzing site usage patterns.
  • Notify you of promotional offers, new product launches, or changes to our policies.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We only share information with third parties involved in order fulfillment (e.g., payment gateways, delivery services) or when legally required.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Cookies help personalize your browsing experience and track website performance. You can manage your cookie preferences through browser settings.

5. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your personal data. While we do our best, no system is entirely immune from threats.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we store.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to external websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests regarding your privacy, please contact us at support@caforia.com.
