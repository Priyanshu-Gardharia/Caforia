Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday to Sunday from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Our kitchen closes at 9:30 PM, but you can still enjoy our beverages until closing time.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We offer delivery through our website and partner with major food delivery platforms. Delivery is available within a 5km radius from our location. Minimum order for delivery is ₹200.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and digital wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay. For online orders, we also accept net banking.

Do you have vegetarian and vegan options?

Absolutely! We have a wide selection of vegetarian options and several vegan-friendly beverages. Our plant-based milk alternatives include almond, soy, and oat milk. Please ask our staff for specific dietary recommendations.

Can I place bulk orders for events?

Yes! We specialize in bulk orders for corporate events, parties, and special occasions. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance for orders above ₹2000. We offer special pricing for bulk orders.

What is your return/refund policy?

If you're not satisfied with your order, please contact us within 30 minutes of receiving it. We'll be happy to replace your item or provide a refund. For delivery orders, please call us directly.

Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes! Join our Caforia Rewards program to earn points on every purchase. Earn 1 point for every ₹10 spent and redeem points for discounts on future orders. Sign up is free!

Can I customize my drink?

Absolutely! We love customizing drinks to your preferences. You can adjust sweetness levels, choose different milk options, add extra shots of espresso, or request special syrups. Just let our baristas know your preferences!
