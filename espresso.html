Barista's Tip

For the perfect espresso, use freshly ground beans and tamp with consistent pressure. The ideal extraction time is 25-30 seconds, creating a rich crema that's the hallmark of a well-crafted shot.

Did you know?

The word "espresso" comes from the Italian word meaning "pressed out" — referring to the high-pressure brewing method that extracts the coffee's full flavor in just 30 seconds.
